Welcome to HerdTrace

Transforming livestock management with technology

HerdTrace is a smart livestock tracking system designed to help farmers protect and manage their animals with ease.

We offer a reliable solution to monitor livestock location and health in real time. Our mobile app provides farmers with updates and alerts, making it easier to track animals, prevent theft, and record important health data.

Whether you’re a commercial or small-scale farmer, HerdTrace is built to be simple, accessible, and effective. For farmers who do not own smartphones, we offer SMS notifications to ensure everyone stays informed and connected.

We believe in using technology to solve real challenges and empower rural communities. With HerdTrace, you gain peace of mind knowing your herd is always one step ahead.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need internet to use HerdTrace?

HerdTrace sends SMS alerts to farmers who do not have smartphones, helping them stay updated without needing internet access.

What animals can I track?

You can track cows, sheep, and goats. We’re expanding to more animals soon.

How does theft detection work?

Using GPS and motion sensors, the system alerts you if livestock moves suspiciously.

Can I register multiple farms?

Yes! HerdTrace allows you to manage several farms and herds in one dashboard.

How much does HerdTrace cost?

There’s a one-time installation fee and a monthly plan based on your herd size.

Is it available in rural areas?

Yes. HerdTrace was designed specifically for rural South African farms, with SMS alerts for remote farmers.

What kind of support does HerdTrace offer?

We offer support via phone, SMS, and WhatsApp. Our team helps you with setup, tracking, and any questions — especially for rural farmers.

Does HerdTrace work on both smartphones and basic phones?

Yes. The HerdTrace app works on smartphones, and we also send SMS alerts for farmers without smartphones — so no one is left out.

Do I need to tag all animals?

No. By applying devices strategically, you can benchmark the herd to receive location, behaiviour and welfare for all animals.
