Our Story & Values

Our Mission

To empower farmers with smart, affordable, technology that protects livestock, prevents theft and improves herd management across rural and commercial farming communities.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading livestock security and tracking platform - creating safer, smarter farms through innovation, data and accessible technology.

Our values

Innovation

We solve farming challenges using the latest technology.

Integrity

Transparency and trust are core to our mission.

Security

Your livestock’s safety is our top priority.

Customer-Centricity

We build for farmers, with farmers.

Our Culture

At HerdTrace, we foster a culture of teamwork, innovation, and inclusivity. As a company proudly founded by passionate women, we are committed to diversity and empowering others in agriculture and technology.
